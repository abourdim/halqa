❓ Help
What is Halqa?

Halqa is a kid-friendly app to learn and use Jitsi Meet video calls for online classes and study circles.

How do I join a meeting?

Enter a room name and your name, then click Join. Share the same room name with others to meet.

How do I change the language?

Open Settings (⚙️) and pick your language. Arabic enables RTL automatically.

Is my data private?

Yes. Participant list and settings are stored locally in your browser. Video calls go through Jitsi servers.

Why is the call limited to 5 minutes?

The free meet.jit.si server limits embedded calls to 5 minutes. To remove this limit: (1) Self-host Jitsi on your own server (free, open source). (2) Use Jitsi as a Service (JaaS) from 8x8.vc (free tier available). (3) Use a community Jitsi server without the limit. You can change the server in Settings.
